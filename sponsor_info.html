Thank you for sponsoring Cal Hacks 2.0! On this page you will find a quick breakdown of your sponsorship benefits and any information you'll need for Cal Hacks 2.0 from October 9-11. We will also be hosting a dinner for all sponsors on Thursday, October 8th for which more information will be emailed out soon. This page will be updated as more information becomes available!

  • Schedule

    The schedule is as follows:
    Setup Begins: Friday, Oct 9 @ 6 PM
    Hacker Registration Begins: Friday, Oct 9 @ 7 PM
    Opening Ceremonies: Friday, Oct 9 @ 10 PM
    Hacking Begins: Friday, Oct 9 @ 11 PM
    Hacking Ends: Sunday, Oct 11 @ 10 AM
    Science Fair Style Project Expo: Sunday, Oct 11 @ 11 AM
    Closing Ceremonies: Sunday, Oct 11 @ 2 PM

  • Location

    Cal Hacks 2.0 will take place at California Memorial Stadium, UC Berkeley's football stadium. The space is stunning and has an excellent view of the entire Bay Area.

    Opening ceremonies, closing ceremonies, and hacking will take place on Goldman Plaza in front of the stadium and in all three of the indoor event spaces (Field Club, Stadium Club, and University Club). We will not be using the field.

    The stadium is located on Piedmont Avenue on the east side of UC Berkeley campus. Here it is on Google Maps. The address is:

    2227 Piedmont Ave
    Berkeley, CA 94720

    The stadium can be accessed by car or by public transit. Parking details will be available soon. For public transit, take the BART to either Downtown Berkeley or Rockridge. You can then take the AC Transit 51B to Bancroft & College Ave. The stadium is about a 5 minute walk up Bancroft Ave from this stop. For those flying in from out of town, both SFO and OAK airports have direct access to BART.

If you have any questions, you can contact me directly at karuna@calhacks.io or (510)640-2082. For details on your sponsorship benefits, click the headers below.

  • Unlimited Mentors and Engineers

    While the number of representatives you can bring from your company (mentors and representatives with engineering background) is unlimited, we recommend bringing no more than five in an attempt to accomodate the hackers and respect venue capacity. Please enter the names and contact details of all of your representatives here.

  • Access to Sponsor Lounge

    The sponsor lounge will be a section reserved for your representatives to take a breather and relax. More details soon.

  • Sponsored API Prize

    We suggest prizes endorsing either a hack category such as Best Hardware Hack, Best Social Hack, or a hack that best utilizes your product. The prizes may be presented to more than 1 team. Hackers seem to be motivated more by prizes such as giant teddy bears, Lego Mindstorms kits, etc. over company coupons or credits.

  • API Demo

    After the opening ceremony, you will have 1-2 minutes to speak on stage to demonstrate your API, talk about your service/product, or give a detailed announcement of what your company does.

  • Distribute Recruiting Materials

    Feel free to surprise our hackers with flyers or pamphlets describing job openings or what you're looking for in an engineer. More details coming soon.

  • Personal Table

    You will receive a table specifically for your company that can be decorated however you wish. You can place swag items, contact information, company banners, etc. The space is yours! It will remain for the entirety of the Hackathon and it does not have to be manned at all times. Dimensions and exact location coming soon.

  • On-Site Interviews

    Details for interviewing hackers coming soon.

  • Resume Book

    After the hackathon, we will link you to our database of we will link you to our database of 1000+ resumes from submitted applications. If there is a special reason you might need it beforehand, please let us know.

  • Sponsor a hacker "Den"

    Hacker Dens are areas of the venue that are laid out specifically for the sponsors to provide support on specific topics (iOS, web development, hardware, etc.). Hackers working or needing help on the topic you choose can then congregate at your den and find help from your engineers. Typically, the topic for the Den you sponsor would be a speciality of your company. If you would like to move your personal table into your Den, please let us know at least two weeks beforehand and we'll be able to accomodate you.

  • Curated Recruiting Lunch/Dinner

    During the hackathon, our top sponsors can have one lunch or dinner with hackers they hand-pick and physically invite through special invitation email templates we can provide for you. More details will be provided later on.

  • Logo on Website & T-Shirt

    Check out your logo on the website here. You'll be able to check out the T-Shirt design soon!

  • Distribute Swag

    Please mail any company swag you'd like to distribute to 266A Sutardja Dai Hall, Berkeley, CA 94720. Some good options for swag items include t-shirts (preferably for both genders), headbands, LED braclets, travel packs, shades, and especially socks!
    If you're shipping pallets of any sort, please ship to: 1923 gridiron way ATTN CAL HACKS 10/11/15-10/13/15 Berkeley CA 94720.

  • Item in all Attendee Swag-bags

    You can include a swag item of your choice with your branding on it. Items can range from coupon codes, to NFC tags, to LED wristbands.

  • Host a Workshop

    Workshops are sessions for you to mentor hackers in your niche topics. If you have some topics that you would be interested in holding, please send them over, along with some preferred time slots so we can fit that into our schedule as soon as possible. We'd be happy to help you brainstorm ideas as well.

  • Sponsored Mini-Event

    Examples of mini-events you could present would be a Code in the Dark challenge, or a lasertag game (as we had last year) to add some more excitement to the hackathon! Again, we'd be happy to help you brainstorm ideas.
